A little about Us

Arogyasaar wishes "संपूर्ण स्वास्थ्य " to each & every individual.

Ayurveda is formed by two words- Ayu and Veda. Here "Ayu means Life and "Veda is Knowledge". Thus Ayurveda gives you knowledge of life.

This science of life has two basic aims; first to preserve the health of healthy people and second is to treat the illness of unhealthy people. Thus Ayurveda not only maintains the health but also cures diseases to retain it. Health is quoted as Swasthya in Ayurveda & gives complete definition of स्वस्थ (healthy person) as follows -


Our website offers you an online consultation and gives you an opportunity to discuss your each and every health issue with qualified & expert physicians. You don't have to wait for hours or days to consult, you can contact us the moment you feel unwell. No matter where you are, no boundaries of locations, you can stay connected with us for betterment of your health. Though online consultation is appreciable way of communication, but for the best result you can consult us physically at *Kalpaveda Ayurved Clinic and Aushadi Bhandar.* Here we are able to examine you thoroughly and perform Nadi-parikshan for accurate diagnosis. Thus ultimately resulting in your best holistic health.
